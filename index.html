ABOUT

Hi, I'm Aravind Mohan.

I'm driven by curiosity. I default to taking things apart to understand how they actually work, then building my own version, usually rougher around the edges than the original, but mine. That instinct has shaped pretty much everything I've worked on, whether it's a full-stack product or the AI systems, agents especially, running underneath it.

I did my Master's in Computer Science at the University at Buffalo, specializing in AI/ML, which is where the pull toward agents and applied AI really took hold. Before that, I worked at Siemens as a software engineer. The job was closer to product engineering: building software that large industrial teams actually used, for clients including BMW, Tesla, and Audi. Since then I've moved through full-stack and applied AI roles at smaller, earlier-stage teams, the kind of environment where you end up wearing a lot of hats and learning fast because there's no one else to hand things off to.

That's the environment I want more of. I like being close to the whole problem, not just my slice of it: the agent logic, the backend it runs on, the interface someone actually uses. I'm not chasing a narrow specialty. I want to keep building end to end, keep learning as I go, and get genuinely good at the intersection of full-stack engineering and AI, not just competent at both separately.
